The aim of this case study was to help Danny generate deeper insights for his restaurant. This case study focuses on using  his customers data, especially their visiting patterns, how much money they’ve spent and also which menu items are their favorite, to build stronger customer relationships and ultimately grow his business. 

Recommendations

Based on the analysis:
Introduce targeted promotions for high-value customers.
Promote top-selling items with combo deals.
Encourage earlier membership sign-ups to capture more customer value.
Offer incentives during the first week of joining to boost engagement.
